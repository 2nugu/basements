# Figure: repose_pile_validation

## Purpose
Validates the W2 Klar calibration (α=0.55) across multiple column-collapse
geometries and along the time evolution at the calibration point. Defends
against single-point calibration criticism in paper §6.

## Data Source
- File: repose_pile_validation.csv (panel a); repose_pile.csv (panel b)
- Generated by: plot_repose_pile.py
- Date: 2026-05-15

## Axes
### Panel (a)
- X: H/D (initial column aspect ratio) — discrete values 0.5, 0.71, 1.0,
     1.5, 2.0, 3.0
- Y: repose angle (degrees), 0–50

### Panel (b)
- X: time (s), 0–3
- Y_left: height (m), radius (m)
- Y_right: angle_slope_fit (degrees)

## Key Observations
- Panel (a): geometric angle ∈ [35°, 38°] for H/D ≤ 0.71 (intact-frustum
  regime, matches literature dry sand 30–35° within +3° upper-bound bias);
  drops to ~25° for H/D ≥ 1.5 (Lube runout regime).
- Panel (b): peak height drops from 0.10m → 0.05m over 1.5s, settling near
  the calibration angle by t=2.0s.
